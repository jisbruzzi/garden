It´s already been 4 or 5 ays from my last post; C++ , CvsC2 and this great game, Invaders: Corruption are to blame.

The game may look like a generic shooter, but, it´s so addictive; the graphics are such a pleasure for your eyes and the music,  for your ears. Also, everything in the games is procedurally randomized from a seed keyword you give to the game, so the graphics you see in each game are unique.
